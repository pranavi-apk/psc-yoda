The PSC Exam In-Depth

The Putonghua Proficiency Test is a standardized exam consisting of 5 key sections, totaling 100 points.

Part 01 10 Points

Single Syllables (100 Characters)

You must read 100 characters within 3.5 minutes. Grading focuses on the total accuracy of initials, finals, and tones. Missing a tone or mispronouncing a vowel will result in small deductions.

  • 100 characters total
  • Time limit: 3.5 minutes
  • Focus: Standard vowel/consonant precision
Part 02 20 Points

Multi-syllable Words (50 Items)

Focuses on 100 characters arranged into words. This part tests Sandhi (tone changes), neutral tones (轻声), and Erhua (儿化) pronunciations.

  • 50 multi-syllable words
  • Tests: Tone sandhi & natural flow
  • Focus: Smooth transitions between syllables
Part 03 10 Points

Word Choice & Judgment

Tests your internalized grammar. You'll read common phrases or decide the correct measure words, suffixes like 'zi' or 'er', and distinguishing between regional dialects and standard Mandarin.

  • Tests: Lexical & Grammatical accuracy
  • Focus: Measure words & functional grammar
Part 04 30 Points

Reading Aloud (400 Words)

You'll read a pre-selected passage of 400 characters. Points are deducted for pauses, misread words, skipped lines, or poor rhythm. Fluency and volume are critical here.

  • Length: 400 character passage
  • Time limit: 4 minutes
  • Focus: Intonation, rhythm, and expressive flow
Part 05 40 Points

Propositional Speech (3 Minutes)

The most weighted section. You'll choose a topic and speak freely for 3 minutes. The AI coach (and real examiners) look for vocabulary richness, grammatical variety, and absence of slang or hesitation.

  • 3-minute continuous speech
  • Focus: Spontaneous language production
  • Grading: Grammar accuracy & vocabulary depth

How to use our AI components to accelerate your learning journey.

Choose Your Target Level

PSC has different levels (1A, 1B, 2A, etc.). Select the level you want to achieve. Yoda adjusts its "strictness" and scoring thresholds based on this goal.

Practice any section. After recording, Yoda's coach provides a detailed mistake breakdown. Click 'Export PDF' to keep a copy of your technical report.

3
Fix Cantonese Patterns

Most students from Hong Kong struggle with similar patterns (e.g. z/zh, n/l). Use the Mastery Dashboard to see a heatmap of exactly which patterns you need to focus on to reach Level 1.

4
Full Mock Simulations

The "Mock Exam" mode is a full 100-point simulation. Use this once a week to test your stamina and see how you rank against real PSC grading standards.
